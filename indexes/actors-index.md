## How to use this index

When drafting a federal-complaint paragraph, post, or article that focuses on a specific actor, open that actor's `.md` file. Each profile cross-references:

1. **Ledger fact IDs** (F### tags) — verifiable in `Hanson_Master_Fact_Ledger_v1.0.md`
2. **Case KB entries** (with paths to specific `00_KEY.md` files)
3. **Source documents** — primary records (transcripts, letters, court orders)

When a new fact emerges about an actor, add it to:
- The fact ledger (the canonical source)
- That actor's `.md` profile (cross-cutting view)
