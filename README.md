## Folder structure

- `1150/` — filed pleadings and exhibits in the family-law matter, including the responsive filings from opposing counsel and non-party witnesses (Florida Bar, Office of the Attorney General, Samarkos, Hodz).
- `10255/` — filings in the parallel state-mandamus action against the Thirteenth Judicial Circuit (Court Administrator + Chief Judge Sabella + Judges Ayers and Felix), brought to compel disclosure of public records.
- `1D26-0728/` — the First DCA appeal challenging The Florida Bar's no-action disposition of RFA No. 25-6861 (Hanson's verified Bar complaint against Scott Paul Davis).
- `composites/` — exhibit composites and analytical exhibits supporting the above matters.
- `indexes/` — reference indexes: actors, topics, evidence maps, docket matrices, and the master fact ledger.

## Citation

Documents are provided in their as-filed form (or, for indexes/composites, as working-aid materials). For citation in connection with the underlying matters, please refer to the original docket entries in the courts of record.
